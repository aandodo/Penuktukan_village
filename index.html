Lokasi Desa Penuktukan

Desa Penuktukan is a beautiful coastal village located on the northern coast of Bali, renowned for its pristine waters and vibrant marine ecosystem. It offers a serene escape from the bustling southern parts of the island, providing a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.
